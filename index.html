Your server deploys automatically within 60 seconds of payment confirmation. You receive an email with your server IP, panel login and setup guide instantly.
Can I upgrade my plan later?+
Yes, anytime from the client area. Upgrades are prorated — you only pay the difference for remaining days. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What are your cancellation/refund policies?+
We offer a full 24-hour money-back guarantee. Contact support within 24 hours and we'll process your refund — no questions asked.
Can I add custom mods and plugins?+
Absolutely. Full FTP access is included on all plans. Install any mods, plugins, custom jars and scripts through the panel or FTP client.
Do you support Indian payment methods?+
Yes! We support UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe), and cryptocurrency. All prices are in INR.
